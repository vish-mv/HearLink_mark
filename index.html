What is HearLink?

The service we offer is specifically designed to meet your needs.

HearLink is a groundbreaking platform revolutionizing video communication, particularly for individuals with hearing impairments. It offers real-time sign language translation and speech recognition capabilities, ensuring inclusivity and accessibility in digital interactions. With seamless integration into everyday communication, HearLink empowers users to engage meaningfully in conversations, fostering connections and bridging communication gaps like never before.
